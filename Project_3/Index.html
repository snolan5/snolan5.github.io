Christoforos Christoforou

Christoforos Christoforou currently works at the Department of Computer Science, Mathematics and Science, St. John's University. Christoforos does research in Computational Neuroscience, Machine learning, Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining.

Ibn Sina Android

The BCI module is controlling Ibn Sina, an android robot which is located 1400 miles away in the United Arab Emirates, in real-time, through an IP connection

Non Invasive BCI

Signal processing and machine learning are playing a fundamental role in enabling applications of BCI and in many respects, advances in signal processing and computation have helped to lead the way to real utility of noninvasive BCI

Eye Tracking

A new approach for extracting neural-based and eye-gaze-based metrics to predict the population-wide behavior of moviegoers on new movie releases
